**What I did**
- Added `Sources/ViewModels/PlantEditViewModel.swift` — `@MainActor` view model with an
  `add` / `edit(plantID:)` `Mode`. Sources its species list **only** from the injected
  `CareDatabase` (T004); `canSave` requires a non-blank nickname **and** a species that resolves
