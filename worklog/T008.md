  derives `due` from `nextDue` vs injected `now` (reusing T006's `DueStatus`), resolves
  `baseIntervalDays` from the care DB, and projects check-ins into `HistoryItem`s **most-recent
  first**. Structured `HistoryItem` (soil/leaves/watered enums, not strings). `scheduleSummary`
  is an **explicit placeholder** until T009 — "Every ~N days (starting cadence) · <due label>",
  falling back to "Watering schedule coming soon." when the species isn't in the care DB.
  Missing/deleted plant → `loadFailed` + cleared state (no crash).
- **`Sources/Views/PlantDetailView.swift`** (new) — `Form` with Species / Schedule / Check-in
  history sections; a `loadFailed` `ContentUnavailableView`; per-row soil/leaves + a water drop
  when `watered`. Pure presentation; `.onAppear { load() }`.
- **Navigation wiring** — `PlantListView` now uses `NavigationStack(path:)` with
  `NavigationLink(value: item.id)` + `.navigationDestination(for: UUID.self)`; new optional
  `makeDetail` factory (cards stay non-tappable when absent, e.g. T006-only contexts).
  `ContentView` adds `makeDetail` against the shared store. `SPROUT_SCREEN=detail` deep-links the
  first plant's detail.
