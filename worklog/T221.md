## 2026-06-02

### Plan
Replace the old add-then-pick-room ordering (a room `Picker` *inside* the basket) with a
two-step **room-first** flow:

1. **Room step** — choose an existing room, **or** add a new one (reusing the T220
   `RoomEditorView`). Selecting/creating a room pre-selects it and advances.
2. **Plants step** — the basket + species picker, with the chosen room shown in the header,
   so every committed plant inherits that `roomID` + its initial cadence.

Implementation:
- Extend `BasketAddViewModel` with a `Step` (`room`/`plants`), `chooseRoom`, `createRoom`,
  `backToRoomStep` — all room-step logic lives in the testable VM.
- New `AddFlowView.swift` (the container) reusing the basket/species sections; `RoomEditorView`
  promoted to internal so the add flow reuses it. Removes the old `BasketAddView.swift`.
- `PlantListView` "+" now presents `AddFlowView`; new `SPROUT_SCREEN=addflow` deep-link lands on
  the room step. `add`/`basket` advance to the plants step (populated-basket screenshots).
