# T053: Fix SproutSheetHeader title centering

## Done
- **2026-07-20**: Fixed SproutSheetHeader to center the title across the full header width, independent of whether trailing confirm and/or leading cancel controls are present.

## Implementation
Changed the header layout from an HStack with two Spacers bracketing the title (which caused the title to center only in the space between controls) to a ZStack approach:
- The title is rendered as a full-width, centered Text element with `.frame(maxWidth: .infinity)` and `.multilineTextAlignment(.center)`
- Cancel and confirm buttons are layered in an HStack with a Spacer between them, floating to the leading and trailing edges respectively
- All original styling preserved: fonts (SproutFont.display(18) title, SproutFont.body(17) buttons), colors (SproutTheme.ink title, SproutTheme.brandGreen buttons), padding/spacing, capsule handle, confirmEnabled opacity/disabled behavior
