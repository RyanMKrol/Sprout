- **Water now**: droplet icon, 1.0 scale, #2F6B4C tint. Headlines vary by reason (onTargetDry → "right on schedule", driedEarly → "dried out faster", dontDryOut → "let's not let it dry out", droopyDry → "leaves are drooping")

- **Skip**: circleCheck icon, 1.0 scale, #B4832F tint (matches SproutTheme.dueTodayAmber). Headlines interpolate day count (e.g., "Skip today — the soil's still wet. Back in about 3 days.")
- **Monitor**: circleInfo icon, 1.0 scale, #7C8173 tint. Headlines vary by reason (droopyMoist → "droopy leaves but damp soil, check tomorrow")
