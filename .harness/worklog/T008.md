# T008: Card styles worklog

## 2026-07-17

Built `Sources/DesignSystem/Cards.swift` with all required container components:

### Components implemented
- **`.sproutCard(radius:)` modifier**: white fill, radius 20 (parameterizable), hairline border rgba(34,39,31,0.05), card shadow
- **`BentoStyle` enum**: sage/oat variants with color properties (fill, border, title, subtitle)
- **`.bentoTile(_ style:)` modifier**: applies bento styling with radius 24, padding 16, and bento shadow

- **`InfoBanner` view**: soft-green fill rgba(47,107,76,0.10), radius 16, icon + SproutFont.body(13) text in #2A6045
- **`CircularToolbarButton` view**: 42Ø white circle, shadow 0 2 8 rgba(50,55,40,0.1), 16pt icon
- **`SproutFAB` view**: 40Ø brandGreen circle, white plus icon, shadow 0 4 12 rgba(47,107,76,0.3)
