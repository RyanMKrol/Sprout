# T051: Fix unbounded glyphs on plant detail schedule card

## 2026-07-20

### What was done
Fixed the droplet and pencil glyphs on the plant detail schedule card that were rendering unbounded and crushing content to a sliver. Root cause: both glyphs were asset-catalog template images (ChromeIcon.image) sized only with `.font(.system(size:))` and no `.resizable()` or `.frame()` — `.font()` does not size non-SF-Symbol template images, so they rendered unbounded.

**Changes made:**
- `Sources/Views/PlantDetailView.swift` line 156-160: Replaced the droplet's `.font(.system(size: 14, weight: .semibold))` with the resizable pattern: `.resizable().aspectRatio(contentMode: .fit).frame(width: 14, height: 14)`
- `Sources/Views/PlantDetailView.swift` line 175-179: Replaced the pencil's `.font(.system(size: 14))` with the resizable pattern: `.resizable().aspectRatio(contentMode: .fit).frame(width: 14, height: 14)`

Both glyphs now render as 14pt icons within their intended containers (droplet sits inside the 34pt green circle, pencil on the right side of the button).
