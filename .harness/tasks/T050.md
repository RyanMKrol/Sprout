## Overview
The 1.5px green outline on the ghost buttons renders with broken/notched corners because of the SwiftUI anti-pattern `.border(color, width:)` followed by `.cornerRadius(r)`: `.border` draws a square-cornered rectangular stroke, and the later `.cornerRadius()` clips the view to a rounded shape, slicing the straight stroke off at each corner. This is visible on the home CTAs (Add a plant / Check in via SproutGhostButtonStyle) and the 'Add a new room' button in the add-plant sheet (same shared style), plus a latent one-off on the Edit Plant delete-card. A repo-wide grep for `.border(` finds exactly TWO occurrences — SproutGhostButtonStyle (Sources/DesignSystem/Buttons.swift:33-37) and Sources/Views/PlantEditView.swift:165-170 — and both use the same anti-pattern. Fix both by stroking a rounded shape instead.

## Do
Replace the `.border(color, width:)` + `.cornerRadius(r)` anti-pattern with an overlay-stroked RoundedRectangle at BOTH call-sites. (1) In `SproutGhostButtonStyle` (Sources/DesignSystem/Buttons.swift, lines 33-37): keep the `.background(Color.clear)`, then replace the `.border(...)` + `.cornerRadius(18)` with `.overlay(RoundedRectangle(cornerRadius: 18, style: .continuous).stroke(<same green color>, lineWidth: 1.5))` and clip the button shape with `.clipShape(RoundedRectangle(cornerRadius: 18, style: .continuous))` so any background is rounded to match. (2) In Sources/Views/PlantEditView.swift (lines 163-170): the `.background(Color.white.border(...))` + `.cornerRadius(SproutTheme.Radius.row)` becomes a white background clipped to a rounded rectangle with the stroke as an overlay — e.g. `.background(Color.white).overlay(RoundedRectangle(cornerRadius: SproutTheme.Radius.row, style: .continuous).stroke(<same destructive-tint color>, lineWidth: 1)).clipShape(RoundedRectangle(cornerRadius: SproutTheme.Radius.row, style: .continuous))` (order the clip/overlay so the fill is rounded and the stroke sits cleanly on the rounded edge). Preserve the exact existing colors, line widths, and corner radii (18 for the ghost button, SproutTheme.Radius.row for the PlantEditView card) — this is a rendering fix, not a restyle. After the change, no `.border(` may remain anywhere in Sources/.
