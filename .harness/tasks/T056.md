## Overview
Teach the AdaptiveEngine to interpret `LeafState.crispy` (dehydration / under-watering) across the three soil states, add the matching `WateringRecommendation.Reason` cases, render a headline for each new reason in `RecommendationPresentation`, and pin the new decision rows with a unit test. Crispy is a genuine product decision — the exact action / nudge / headline for crispy+dry, crispy+moist, crispy+wet come from the answer to the paired `topic:"other"` question; if that answer is unavailable at build time, use the sensible-default mapping stated below and the reviewing human can adjust the pinned test.
