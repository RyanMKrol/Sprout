## Overview
The shared sheet-header component SproutSheetHeader (Sources/DesignSystem/SheetScaffold.swift, struct at line 3) mis-centres its title. Its bar is an HStack laid out as [optional Cancel button] -> Spacer -> Text(title) -> Spacer -> [optional confirm button] (lines 36-62). The two Spacers centre the title in the space BETWEEN the leading and trailing controls, not across the full bar width. When a sheet has a leading Cancel but NO trailing confirm action (confirmLabel == nil), the empty trailing slot leaves the title centred only in the region to the right of Cancel, so it visibly shifts right. The Add Plants sheet is exactly this case: AddFlowView.swift:217 constructs SproutSheetHeader(title: "Add Plants", ...) with a leading Cancel but a nil confirm label (its confirm action is a full-width button pinned at the sheet bottom, not a header trailing button). Because this is a SHARED component used by many sheets, the fix must centre the title across the FULL header width regardless of which side controls exist, WITHOUT breaking sheets that DO have both a Cancel and a trailing confirm action (e.g. Add Room: Cancel / Add), which look balanced today and must stay balanced.

## Do
Change SproutSheetHeader (Sources/DesignSystem/SheetScaffold.swift) so the title is centred horizontally across the FULL width of the header bar independent of whether the leading cancel and/or trailing confirm controls are present. The idiomatic approach is to render the centred Text(title) as a full-width, centred element (e.g. .frame(maxWidth: .infinity) with .multilineTextAlignment(.center), or as a ZStack/overlay layer) and float the Cancel button to the leading edge and the confirm button to the trailing edge OVER (or around) it, rather than relying on two Spacers to bracket the title. Keep the existing capsule grab-handle, fonts (SproutFont.display(18) title, SproutFont.body(17) buttons), colours (SproutTheme.ink title, SproutTheme.brandGreen buttons), spacing/padding (.padding(.horizontal, 20), .padding(.vertical, 16)), the confirmEnabled opacity/disabled behaviour, and the optional-control semantics (cancelLabel/confirmLabel may each be nil). Do NOT change the component's public initialiser signature or its call sites — only its internal layout. Do NOT touch any file outside Sources/DesignSystem/SheetScaffold.swift.
