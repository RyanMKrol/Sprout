# T013 — Unified check-in outcome voice (RecommendationPresentation)

## Overview
One presentation mapping from the engine's check-in outcomes to the redesign's icon + tint +
"{Action} — {reason}." headline, consumed later by the check-in sheet and guided watering.

   the shape "{Action} — {reason}." — reuse the engine's reason text where the table says so.

   including "Water now — right on schedule." and the skip case's tint == #B4832F equivalent
   (compare to SproutTheme.dueTodayAmber).
